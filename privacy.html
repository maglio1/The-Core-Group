Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Core Group ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

When you fill out our contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and message. We don't collect this information unless you send it to us directly.

Our website may use basic analytics (such as page views and referral sources) to understand how visitors find us. We don't use tracking cookies for advertising, and we don't sell or share your data with advertisers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, follow up about our services, and communicate with you about your project if you become a client. That's it. We don't add you to marketing lists without your permission, and we don't send unsolicited promotional emails.

Who We Share It With

We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We may use third-party tools to process form submissions (such as Formspree) or schedule calls (such as Acuity Scheduling). These services have their own privacy policies and are only used to facilitate your request.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including using HTTPS encryption on our website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You can ask us to delete any personal information we have about you at any time. Just email or call us and we'll take care of it. If you're a Virginia resident, you have additional rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act, including the right to access, correct, and delete your data.

Children

Our website and services aren't directed at anyone under 18. We don't knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We won't make major changes without giving you a heads up if you're an existing client.
